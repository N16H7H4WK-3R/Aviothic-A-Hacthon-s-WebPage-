Welcome to Aviothic 1.0
A 12-hour hackathon that brings together programmers, designers, and inventors to collaborate on coding, designing, and building inventive and promising solutions to address real-world issues
This is a venue for self-expression and creativity through technology.


Whether a person is creating their first app or their hundredth, there is always a lot to learn. This presents an opportunity for programmers to enhance their technical skills and develop their ability to think creatively, regardless of their level of experience.
The only limitation is the amount of time available to them!
